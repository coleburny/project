Game of Codes Presents:

Snake MMO

By: Justin Piazza (Front-End), Cole Burniston (Server), and Christian Scholl (Back-End)




Rules of the Game:

1). Enter a Username, then press submit to pair up with a snake

2). Click "Start Game"

3). Once you are in the game, you will have 10 seconds to get ready before the game starts

4). When the timer reaches 1:00, you will compete against other players to collect as many dots as possible

5). When the timer reaches 0:00, the game has ended
